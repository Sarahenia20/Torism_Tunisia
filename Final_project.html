What’s the best time of year to visit Tunisia?

Tunisia is a year-round destination. Winters are great for outdoor activities like downhill skiing in the north side or visiting our charming Sahara. Spring and autumn are the best seasons to join some agriculture related festivals and rituals . Late summer and autumn are ideal for sightseeing and enjoying colorful autumn foliage.Also luxurious hotels and resorts are open all year year-round .

Do I need a passport or visa to enter the country?

You need a valid national passport or another equivalent that satisfactorily establishes your identity and nationality. If you are not a citizen of Tunisia, you may also need a visa in some instances. Please check with your local Tunisian Embassy, and they’ll be happy to help you.

What are some basic facts about Tunisia?

Tunisia is a Muslim country that’s located in north africa by the Mediterranean sea. It is famous for beautiful pottery, ironwork, and mosaics. Tunisian people are generally relaxed and sociable, but also emphasize showing respect for one another, especially their elders.

Yes it is!! it's called "Slata Mechwia" or roasted bell pepper salas usually made with roasted tomatoes garlic and topped with boiled eggs and canned Tuna.
